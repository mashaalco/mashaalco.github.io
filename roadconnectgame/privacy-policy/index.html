Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 19, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how our mobile application (the "Road Connect: Slide 15 Puzzle"), a simple puzzle game, collects, uses, and shares information when you use our services.

We do not directly collect, store, or share any personal data from our users. However, the App integrates a third-party advertising SDK, Google Mobile Ads (AdMob), which automatically collects and processes certain user data to serve advertisements, prevent fraud, and analyze app performance.

1. Information Collected Automatically by Third Parties

By using this App, the Google Mobile Ads (AdMob) SDK may automatically collect and process the following data types:

  • Approximate Location: Data about your general location (such as city or country) derived from your IP address, used to serve relevant localized ads.
  • App Interactions: Information about how you interact with advertisements inside the app (such as ad clicks, impressions, or video views).
  • Diagnostics and Performance Data: Technical logs, crash reports, and app performance metrics used to identify bugs and maintain app stability.
  • Device or Other IDs: Unique identifiers such as your Android Advertising ID (AAID), IP address, and device model, used for ad personalization, frequency capping, and security.

2. How Your Information is Used

The data collected by AdMob is utilized for the following core purposes:

  • Advertising & Marketing: To deliver, target, and measure the effectiveness of advertisements shown within the puzzle game.
  • Analytics: To understand how the app performs, monitor crash rates, and improve the overall user experience.
  • Fraud Prevention & Security: To detect malicious activity, prevent ad abuse, and ensure a secure environment for our users.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell or directly share your personal data with anyone. However, the data collected automatically by the AdMob SDK is shared directly with Google and its advertising partners in accordance with Google's privacy policies.

You can review Google’s privacy practices by visiting:

  • Google Partner Privacy Policy
  • Google Play Data Safety Disclosures for AdMob

4. User Choice and Opt-Out

Because the AdMob SDK initializes automatically when the app is launched, data collection is required to use the App. However, you can manage or limit personalized advertising directly through your device settings:

  • Android Devices: Go to Settings > Privacy > Ads and select "Reset advertising ID" or "Delete advertising ID" to limit cross-app tracking.

5. Children's Privacy

Our App is designed for a general audience and is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe that a child under 13 has provided data through the AdMob SDK in our app, please contact Google or adjust your device's privacy controls.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Effective Date" at the top.
